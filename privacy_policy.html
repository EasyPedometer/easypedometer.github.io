This Privacy Policy has been created to disclose our information collection, use and disclosure practices for the Blood Pressure Tracker ("Health") application.

This health product integrates advanced health monitoring features, including blood pressure tracking, heart rate measurement, blood glucose monitoring, step counting, and water intake recording. It is designed to provide users with comprehensive health management support. Through precise data analysis and real-time feedback, helping users better understand and maintain their health.

What Information Do We Collect?

When you use and interact with our service, we collect the following type of information (including personal data) is for the many purposes, specifically:

1. Functional information processed directly:

  • Health Data: Users voluntarily enter health data, including blood sugar levels and blood pressure readings, specifically Systolic (SYS), Diastolic (DIA), and Blood Pressure Level (BUL) metrics.

  • Profile Information: Additionally, users may provide personal profile information, which comprises: Full Name, Year of Birth, Gender, Pregnancy Status Information, Diabetes Under Treatment Status Information (We have removed this feature in the latest version to ensure the highest level of data security for your profile.)

  • Data Storage and Use: All health and profile data is retained locally on the user's device. The primary purpose of collecting this data is to facilitate the main function: record blood sugar and blood pressure, generation and exportation of reports within the app. The data is not transmitted beyond the local device environment under any circumstances.

  • Data Deletion Procedures: Users maintain the right to delete all of their stored data at any time. This can be accomplished simply by uninstalling the application. Upon uninstallation, all data—including health metrics and personal profile information—will be irreversibly and automatically expunged from the device.
